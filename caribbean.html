The Challenge

Why Most Caribbean Businesses
Fail to Scale

Operational Chaos

Manual processes, undocumented workflows, and ad-hoc decision making create bottlenecks that prevent growth.

Owner Dependency

Businesses that rely entirely on the founder cannot scale, sell, or survive leadership transitions.

Data Blindness

Lack of KPI tracking and analytics means decisions are based on intuition rather than measurable insights.

Legacy Systems

Outdated technology stacks that are expensive to maintain and impossible to integrate with modern tools.

Low Valuation

Without proper structuring and systems, businesses sell for fractions of their potential market value.

Talent Gaps

Difficulty attracting and retaining talent due to unstructured roles and lack of professional development paths.
